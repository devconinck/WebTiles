What are Bangs?

Bangs are shortcuts that let you quickly search on other sites directly from your search bar. By typing a bang (an exclamation mark followed by a keyword), you can instantly redirect your search to a specific website.

For example, try these popular bangs:

  • !t3 — Search on T3 Chat
  • !gt — Search on Google Translate
  • !a — Search on Amazon

You can find a full list of available bangs on the DuckDuckGo Bangs page.

Note: If you do not use a bang, your search will default to Google.
